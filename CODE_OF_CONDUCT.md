# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in Fuji a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, gender, identity, or background.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Being respectful and inclusive
- Giving constructive feedback
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and are expected to take appropriate action in response to any unacceptable behavior.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
